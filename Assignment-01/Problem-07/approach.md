# Approach for the getting Average of 3 numbers and finding Numbers that are Greater and Lesser than it

Let's say the three given numbers are stored in variable $a$, $b$, $c$. Now take another variable $avg$ tha'll store the average of the three numbers. We know average of $n$ numbers is:
$$
\text{Average} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{r=1}^{n} a_r
$$
$a_r$ are the $r$ th number.
So for 3 numbers, the average will be:
$$
\text{Average}=\frac{1}{3} (a+b+c)
$$
Next we check if $avg$ is greater than or lesser than $a$, $b$, $c$.
